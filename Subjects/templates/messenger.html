Publish your Messenger Groupchat to UniChat

Messenger Groupchats do not have the capability to add users via a sharable link. This has been an issue for groupchat moderators for a few years now :(

Never fear, UniChat is here to help. UniChat will allow people to request access to your groupchat, and give you a list of those who have requested to be added so you can add them at your convinience.
